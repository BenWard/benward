Ben Ward

I'm Ben Michael Ward (born February 1984), a software engineer and web developer from Cambridge, UK. I graduated in Computation at the University of Manchester Institute of Science and Technology and am currently employed by Twitter Twitter, in San Francisco.

I've been doing things with the internet for over a decade, and as well as writing software have also written for various online publications.

My earliest memory is aged three, laying length-ways along a flight of stairs and attempting to roll down them one at a time. My earliest missing memory is what happened next.

For a more thorough professional summary, can read my curriculum vitæ.

This site

This site is maintained and hosted by myself, with help from the very lightweight Jekyll CMS. It's a stock install, with various hacks and additions. You can learn about how it works by looking at the source code on Github.

I believe that you, the person reading or listening to this, should be able to resize it, re-colour it, rearrange it and mess with it to your personal satisfaction using whatever client-side technology is available to you. The ‘pixel perfect internet’ does not and shan't ever exist, so we shouldn't try to fake it.
