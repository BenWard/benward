[LastGraph](lastgraph.aeracode.org/) — a gorgeous app to colourfully graph your Last.FM listening habits — allows you to set the detail level of your graph. The choices are:

> * Super
> * High
> * Medium (checked)
> * Low
> * Terrible
> * Abysmal
> * Excruciatingly Bad
